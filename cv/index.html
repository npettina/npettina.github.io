Publications

  • 2020.08.13
    Higher-Order Interactions Dampen Pairwise Competition in the Zebrafish Gut Microbiome
    Mbio
    We examined pairwise species interactions among commensal intestinal microbes from larval zebrafish. We found that with multiple species present, interbacterial interactions are weaker, highlighting the importance of higher-order interactions in the vertebrate gut to stabilize microbial communities.
